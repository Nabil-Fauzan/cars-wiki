Legacy & Heritage

The GT-800 Interceptor represents the pinnacle of four decades of endurance racing experience. Born from the secret labs of the PCAR Performance Division, this machine was initially designed as a technological demonstrator for active aerodynamics and kinetic energy recovery systems. The chassis is a monolithic carbon-fiber structure, a first for a production vehicle of this class, ensuring unparalleled rigidity and weight distribution.
